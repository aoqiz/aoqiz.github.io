Biography

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, the University of Melbourne (UoM), under the supervision of Dr. Mingming Gong and Dr. Susan Wei. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Sichuan University in 2018 and M.S. degree in Data Science from University of Melbourne in 2020. My research mainly focuses on causal inference and its applications to fair and trustworthy machine learning. In addition, I am also interested in exploring large language models (LLMs) through the lens of causal reasoning.

From February to October 2024, I had the opportunity to join the University of Amsterdam as a visiting Ph.D. student, where I worked with Dr. Sara Magliacane at the AMLab .
